Australia's Premier Crypto Event Returns for 2025

PARTNER CONTENT · 4 MIN READ · APRIL 15, 2025
SHARE

Australia's largest blockchain and Web3 conference is returning for its biggest edition yet. Over three days in Sydney, more than 5,000 attendees will hear from global exchange executives, DeFi protocol founders, regulatory officials, and institutional investors navigating the post-ETF landscape.

EVENT HIGHLIGHTS — 2025 EDITION
5,000+Registered Attendees
120+Speakers on Stage
3Days in Sydney
40+Sponsoring Partners

What to Expect at This Year's Event

The 2025 edition expands to three full days, with tracks dedicated to institutional adoption, DeFi infrastructure, regulatory compliance, and Web3 gaming. Keynote sessions will feature executives from major exchanges alongside policymakers from ASIC and Treasury officials monitoring digital asset frameworks.

A dedicated startup pitch competition returns, giving early-stage blockchain projects direct exposure to venture capital partners from Australia, Singapore, and Hong Kong. Side events throughout the week include private roundtables on stablecoin regulation and tokenized asset frameworks.

Confirmed Speaker Categories

Exchange CEOs, Layer 1 and Layer 2 protocol founders, institutional asset managers managing crypto allocations, regulatory representatives from multiple Asia-Pacific jurisdictions, and Web3 infrastructure builders are all confirmed for keynote and panel sessions.

The event organizers have prioritized speakers who operate at the intersection of traditional finance and decentralized markets — reflecting the post-ETF landscape where institutional and retail narratives have converged.

Registration and Venue Details

The event takes place at the International Convention Centre Sydney. Early-bird registration is open through the official event website. Media accreditation for journalists and content creators is available on a separate track. Sponsorship and exhibition booth opportunities are available for companies seeking exposure to the region's most concentrated crypto audience.

TheCCPress is a media partner for the 2025 edition. All editorial coverage of speakers and sessions remains fully independent from the commercial partnership arrangement.
